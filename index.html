Many Random Numbers

This is the final project in my Graduate Research Seminar course. We were assigned to explore the behavior of a Pascal random number generator throwing large number of random numbers and collecting them equally into histogram bins. This was to demonstrate the phenomena known as ‘Law of large numbers’. We then had to present this empirically generated data in forms of graphs and explanations. Using this voice-over PPT, I presented the data generated in this project.
